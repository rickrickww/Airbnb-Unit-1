Fares & Compass Card

Transit in Metro Vancouver uses a Compass Card — a reloadable tap card used on SkyTrain, bus, and SeaBus.

You can buy and load a Compass Card at any SkyTrain station (including Bridgeport) from the fare vending machines. A card costs $6 deposit and you load credit onto it.

You can also tap to pay with a credit or debit card directly at fare gates — no Compass Card needed.

Compass Card fare (adult) ~$2.55–$3.10
Day Pass $11.25

Fares may vary. Check translink.ca for current pricing.
